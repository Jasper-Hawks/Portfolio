Completion of the TODO list, timers and Pysistant

6/28/2021

Pysistant and the rest of its features are fully completed.

Problems and Solutions

The majority of the problems were mostly formatting oversights such as putting a newline character in an incorrect place. Overall this portion of the project was the easiest out of the Calculator and Dictionary

Pysistant proved to be a fun and frustrating project. There were areas such as the TODO list that were challenging where stepping back and taking a break always revealed a simple solution to an aggravating problem. This was a great way of combining my knowledge of python fundamentals and libraries into a project that I will use everyday. You can check out the repository here.
